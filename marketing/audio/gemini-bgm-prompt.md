# Gemini Looping BGM Prompt

Create an original seamless looping background music track for a fantasy digital trading card game called Dragon Cards.

Style: strategic fantasy card duel, elemental dragons, mystical but not dark, polished indie game prototype. Blend orchestral fantasy pads, light hand percussion, soft taiko-style pulses, warm low strings, airy choir texture without lyrics, and subtle crystalline magical accents. The music should support thinking and deck strategy, not overpower gameplay.

Mood: focused, heroic, ancient, tactical, inviting, slightly mysterious. It should feel good under menus, deck building, and card battles.

Loop requirements: 90 seconds, seamless loop, no hard ending, no long intro, no sudden dropouts, no vocals, no copyrighted melodies, no recognizable references to existing games or TCGs.

Tempo and key: 92 BPM, D minor with hopeful modal color. Keep percussion restrained and make the main motif memorable but subtle.

Mix: clean stereo, game-ready, moderate dynamics, leave room for UI and card SFX, no heavy bass, no harsh high end.

Export target: high-quality WAV, loopable, suitable for conversion into OGG for a Windows desktop game.
